Hwiyo Hwiyo Mobile Application

  • Problem 90% of homeowners in Zimbabwe dont have the time outside of their daily schedules to go and make payments or purchases for utilities.
  • Goal Design an app that will enable clients to purchase and pay their utilities.

  • Research

    I developed interview questions using data from various Utility regulatory bodies before conducting user interviews. The majority of interviewees expressed time and convenience as their top priority when it comes to purchasing and making payments for utilities. Users would be receptive and willing to try a new platform provided it addresses their concerns.

    PERSONAS

  • Ideation

    I did a quick ideation exercise to come up with ideas for how to address gaps identified in the competitive audit.

  • Initial

    After ideating and drafting some paper wireframes, I created the initial designs for the Utility Bills purchasing app. These designs focused on helping the user purchase their utility bills .

  • Key insights

    I conducted two rounds of usability studies. Findings from the first study helped guide the designs from wireframes to mockups. The second study used a high-fidelity prototype and revealed what aspects of the mockups needed refining.
    1. People wanted an easy way to view the outcome of the transaction
    2. People had difficulty searching for specific transactions
    3. People had difficulty on getting started on the application

  • Solution

    Based on the insights from the usability studies, I applied design changes to address the key insights that where discovered during the usability study
